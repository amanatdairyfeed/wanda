Privacy Policy

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Welcome to Amanat Dairy Feed. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and act upon the information you provide to us through our website. By using our site, you agree to the terms laid out in this policy.

We are committed to full compliance with local data protection laws of Pakistan.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our Dealer Finder or Contact Form, we may ask for:

Your data is stored securely on our encrypted servers. We use your information solely for:

You have the right to request the deletion of your data from our records at any time. To exercise this right, please contact us at info@amanatfeed.com.
