Our Legacy of Excellence

Leading the dairy feed industry in Pakistan with integrity, innovation, and quality.

Decades of Trust

Established in Kasur, the hub of dairy farming in Pakistan, Amanat Dairy Feed (Pvt) Ltd. began with a vision to revolutionize cattle nutrition. We saw a gap in the market for high-quality, scientifically formulated feed that could genuinely improve yield without compromising animal health.

From a small manufacturing unit, we have grown into a large-scale industrial operation. Today, our products are trusted by thousands of farmers across Punjab and beyond. Our commitment to using toxin-free ingredients and maintaining strict quality control has earned us a reputation as the "Farmer's Best Friend".
