State-of-the-Art Laboratory

Our on-site laboratory is equipped with the latest Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (NIR) technology, allowing us to analyze raw materials and finished products within seconds.

From measuring moisture content to detecting microscopic toxin levels, our qualified chemists leave no stone unturned. This rigorous testing ensures that your animals receive safe feed free from aflatoxins and pesticide residues.

What We Test For

Moisture Control

Excess moisture leads to mold. We keep it strictly below 13%.

Aflatoxin Screening

Checking for fungal toxins that contaminate milk.

Protein Analysis

Verifying Crude Protein (CP) levels match product guarantees.

Amanat Dairy Feed collaborates with the University of Veterinary and Animal Sciences (UVAS) to conduct regular trials. Our R&D team works tirelessly to improve formulas, enhance palatability, and introduce cost-effective solutions for farmers.
